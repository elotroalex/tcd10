The Caribbean Digital conference (TCD) is an anual international event, held in locations in the United States and the Caribbean since 2014. After a pandemic hiatus, this year the annual Caribbean Digital event will be held in person at the University of Puerto Rico in Rio Piedras. On November 17, we will come together for a two-hour workshop on Collection Data and Caribbean Cultural Heritage. On Friday, November 18, we will convene two panels and a series of activities highlighting the work of cultural heritage, data as memory and community groups on the ground doing the work.
